Frequently Asked Questions

What is dark web monitoring?

expand_more
Dark web monitoring is a service that searches the hidden parts of the internet where stolen data is often traded. BreachWatch identifies if your email, passwords, or other personal identifiers have been compromised.

How does BreachWatch check breaches?

expand_more
We aggregate billions of records from hundreds of historical and recent data breaches. When you enter your email, we compare it against our encrypted database of known leaks to find matches.

Is my email stored?

expand_more
No. Privacy is our top priority. We do not store your email addresses during the search process. We only use them to query our database and provide you with instant results.

What should I do if my email is leaked?

expand_more
If a leak is detected, we recommend immediately changing your password for that service and any other accounts that share the same credentials. Enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) is also highly recommended.
